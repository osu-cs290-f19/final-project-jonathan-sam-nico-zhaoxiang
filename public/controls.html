How to Play:

  • Up Arrow - Accelerate forwards
  • Down Arrow - Accelerate backwards
  • Right Arrow - Turn clockwise
  • Left Arrow - Turn counter-clockwise
  • Spacebar - Shoot!

Goal of the Game:

The goal is to survive the endless asteroid field. Your progress is tracked by your score, and the level. Asteroids get faster with each level.

Power Ups:

Power-ups are essential for your survival, granting boons to your ship attributes. Pick them up by flying into them.
