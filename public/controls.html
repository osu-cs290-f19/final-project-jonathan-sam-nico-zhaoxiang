How to Play:
  • Up Arrow - Moves starship upwards
  • Down Arrow - Moves starship downwards
  • Right Arrow - Turns starship clock-wise
  • Left Arrow - Turns starchip counter-clock-wise
  • Spacebar - Shoots the artillery in the starship
Goal of the Game:
The goal of the game is rather simple, all you need to do is survive.
Power Ups:
Picking up the power-ups will give you a benefit that will let you survive for longer, an example of a power-up would be the increase in shooting spread
